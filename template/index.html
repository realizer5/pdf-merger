PDF Merger

Merge PDF Files

Drag and drop your PDF files here, or click to select files.

Specify Pages to Merge

Enter the pages you want to merge. You can specify individual pages (e.g., 1,3,5) or a page range (e.g., 4-10). Leave empty to merge all pages
Examples:
- To merge specific pages: 1,3,5
- To merge a range of pages: 4-10 (this merges pages 4 through 10)
Note: Make sure pages are separated by commas, and use a hyphen for ranges.
